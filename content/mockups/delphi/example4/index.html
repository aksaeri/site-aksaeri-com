How to Read This Chart

Each line represents a risk's probability trajectory across severity levels. Steeper drops indicate risks that are less likely to reach catastrophic levels, while flatter lines suggest persistent tail risks.

Risk 3.1 (highlighted) shows an 85% probability of at least minor harm, dropping to 50% for moderate harm and 12% for catastrophic outcomes.
